Thank you for your application for an S1.

We need you to send us some evidence so we can process your application.

What you need to send

You need to send us a letter from the Department for Work and Pensions (DWP) that shows the exact date you started getting paid your UK State Pension.

We cannot process your application until we get this evidence.

Send your evidence online

You can send us your evidence by taking a photo of it with a mobile phone or digital camera, or by scanning it.

{{ insetText({ "HTML": "

Send us your evidence online at https://services.nhsbsa.nhs.uk/cra/upload

" }) }}

You’ll need to give us your:

  • National Insurance number
  • OHS reference number
  • name
  • date of birth

This link will expire in 12 weeks. If you have not sent your evidence before {{ '22 May 2024' | twelveWeeksFromNow }}, you’ll need to contact us to ask for a new link.

If you cannot send your evidence online, let us know by email at S1applications@nhsbsa.nhs.uk

{% include '../apply/_sla.html' %}

If you do not have this evidence

If the DWP cannot give you a letter with this information, you can ask them to send us an email notification called a HINT. This will give us the details we need. Find out how to contact the DWP on the GOV.UK website.

If you know when the DWP sent us a HINT, tell us and we will look for it.
