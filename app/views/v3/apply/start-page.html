Use this service to apply for a certificate of entitlement called an S1 to get healthcare cover for living abroad.

You can also contact us to get help with your application or apply by phone.

What an S1 covers

An S1 entitles you to the same state provided healthcare cover as a resident of the country you’re living in. It does not cover private healthcare.

Who can apply online

To apply for an S1 online, you must be:

  • living in or moving to an EU country in the next 90 days
  • getting paid your UK State Pension or entitled to start getting paid your UK State Pension in the next 90 days

If you’re not sure if you can apply, check the NHS Business Services Authority website for more information about S1 eligibility.

Add family members to your application

You can add family members to your application as dependants, including your:

  • spouse or civil partner
  • partner
  • children

If a family member is entitled to an S1 in their own right, they must make a separate application.

The country you’re moving to will tell you if they accept your family members as dependants when you register your S1 with them.

Before you start

You’ll need:

  • an email address – if you do not have an email address, you’ll need to contact us to apply
  • your National Insurance number
  • an address in the country you’re moving to
  • dates of birth and addresses for any family members you add to your application

Evidence

We’ll tell you if you need to send us any evidence after you submit your online application.
