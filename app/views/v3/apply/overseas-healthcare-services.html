Overseas Healthcare Services

We manage reciprocal healthcare for people travelling to and from most European countries on behalf of the Department of Health and Social Care (DHSC).

If you need medical treatment outside the UK, you may be entitled to:

  • a UK Global Health Insurance Card (GHIC)
  • a UK European Health Insurance Card (EHIC)
  • a Provisional Replacement Certificate (PRC)
  • an S1
  • an S2
