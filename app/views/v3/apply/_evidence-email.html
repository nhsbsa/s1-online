Sending your evidence

You can send us your evidence by taking a photo of it with a mobile phone or digital camera, or by scanning it.

{{ insetText({ "HTML": "

Send the photo or scanned image as an email attachment to S1applications@nhsbsa.nhs.uk

" }) }}

You must include these details in your email:

  • OHS reference number
  • full name
  • date of birth
  • the address you used in your application

If you cannot send your evidence this way, let us know by email at S1applications@nhsbsa.nhs.uk
