Claim a refund for treatment costs

If you have paid for medically necessary state-provided healthcare whilst visiting an EU country, Switzerland, Norway, Iceland or Liechtenstein, you may be able to claim a refund of your costs. Any refund is subject to eligibility.

Not all state-provided healthcare is free within these countries and you may be required to pay for services which are usually provided for free in the UK.

Visit the Claim a refund for treatment costs page for more information about:

  • how to apply
  • what you can claim a refund for
  • what information you’ll need to provide

If you have travelled to receive pre-planned treatment using an S2, refer to the S2 section on this page for more information.

Report suspected fraud

If you suspect that somebody has fraudulently applied for or used a UK GHIC or UK EHIC they are not entitled to, you can report this to the NHS Counter Fraud Authority.

Alternatively, you can email your concern to us at lcfs@nhsbsa.nhs.uk.
