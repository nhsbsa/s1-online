What does an S1 form cover?

An S1 form entitles you to the same state provided health cover as a resident of the country you’re moving to.

It does not cover private healthcare.

Who can use this online service?

To apply for an S1 form online, you must be:

  • permanently living in or moving to an EU country in the next 90 days
  • getting paid your UK State Pension or entitled to start getting paid your UK State Pension in the next 90 days

If you are moving to Switzerland, Norway, Iceland or Liechtenstein, you may be eligible for an S1 form, but you cannot apply online. You’ll need to contact us to apply.

If you think you are entitled to an S1 form but you’re not getting paid your UK State Pension, you can find out about planning your healthcare cover abroad on our website.

Family members

You can add family members to your application as dependants, including your:

  • spouse or civil partner
  • partner
  • children

If a family member is entitled to an S1 in their own right, they must make a separate application.

The country you’re moving to will tell you if they accept your family members as dependants when you register your S1 form with them.

Before you start

You’ll need:

  • an email address
  • your National Insurance number
  • an address in the country you’re moving to
  • dates of birth and addresses for any family members you add to your application

Evidence

We will tell you if you need to send us any evidence after you submit your online application.
