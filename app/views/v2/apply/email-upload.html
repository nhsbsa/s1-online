Thank you for your application for an S1 form.

We need you to send us some evidence so we can process your application.

Your UK State Pension start date.

When you apply for an S1, there is an automatic check to the Department for Work and Pensions (DWP) to confirm the date your UK State Pension started.

This check was not able to confirm your UK State Pension start date.

We need you to send us a letter from the DWP that shows the exact date your pension was first paid.

If the DWP cannot give you a letter with this information, you can ask them to send us an email notification called a HINT. This will give us the pension details we need. Find out how to contact the DWP on the GOV.UK website.

If you know when the DWP sent us a HINT, tell us and we will look for it.

Sending your evidence

You can send us your evidence by taking a photo of it with a mobile phone or digital camera, or by scanning it.
