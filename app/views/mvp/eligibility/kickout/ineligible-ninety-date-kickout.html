You cannot apply for an S1 for healthcare cover

To be entitled to an S1 for healthcare cover, you must be either:

  • permanently living in an EU country, Switzerland, Norway, Iceland, or Liechtenstein
  • permanently moving to an EU country, Switzerland, Norway, Iceland, or Liechtenstein in the next 90 days
