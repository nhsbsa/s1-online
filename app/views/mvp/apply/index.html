Apply for an S1 form

Apply for a certificate of entitlement called an S1 form to get healthcare cover when living or moving abroad.

Important:

You must have an address in the country you’re moving to for an S1 form to be issued.

This can be a temporary address but if it changes, you must tell the NHS Business Services Authority (NHSBSA) and the relevant authority in the country you’re moving to.

We can send the form to your UK address if you need it for your visa application.

What does an S1 form cover?

An S1 form entitles you to state provided healthcare in the country you’re moving to.

It does not cover private healthcare.

Who can use this online service?

To apply for an S1 form online, you must be:

  • permanently living in or moving to an EU country in the next 90 days
  • getting paid your UK State Pension or entitled to start getting paid your UK State Pension in the next 90 days

If you are moving to Switzerland, Norway, Iceland or Liechtenstein, you may be eligible for an S1 form, but you cannot apply online. Please contact us to apply.

You can apply for yourself or make an application for someone else.

Dependants

You can add dependants to your application including your:

  • spouse or civil partner
  • partner
  • children

The country you’re moving to will tell you if they accept your dependants when you register your S1 form with them.

Before you start

You’ll need:

  • your National Insurance number
  • an address in the country you’re moving to
  • dates of birth and addresses for any dependants you add to your application

Evidence

We will tell you if you need to send any evidence after you apply.
